Beyond Research

Music, Photography, and Outdoor Curiosity

This page keeps a little distance from the academic portfolio, but remains easy to find. These interests shape how I observe details, collaborate with others, and keep a steady rhythm outside project work.

Music

Performance and Collaboration

Music has been one of my most social interests. I have performed in university-level large-stage events with thousands of participants, and have joined exchange activities with Stanford's The Harmonics and Yale's Whiffenpoofs. Performance settings train attention, timing, listening, and the ability to support a group while still holding a clear part.

Photography

Looking Closely

Photography is a quiet way to practice composition, patience, and visual judgment. It also connects naturally with my interest in imaging and how scenes become interpretable records.

Sport

Orienteering and Endurance

Outdoor activity gives me a useful counterweight to screen-based work: spatial awareness, pacing, and decisions made under changing conditions.
